SPORTS STORE

SportsStore Client Side Applications

The client side of the SportsStore application consists of two separate AngularJS applications:
  • The Customer Store: where a customer can browse and select items, add them to a shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
  • The Administration Site: where registered users and administrators can browse orders and maintain the products catalogue.

Note

The SportsStore client applications require the server side to be running.Visit http://localhost:9001 to check it.
